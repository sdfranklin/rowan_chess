Rules

Standard Web Version

This page uses the standard rules only. The respawn variant is not on the website yet.

The Board

  • The board has 5 columns and 7 playable seam lines.
  • Pieces sit on the horizontal seams, not inside the tiles.
  • At the start, each side secretly chooses which starting column is left empty.

Pawns

  • Pawns move any number of spaces horizontally or vertically through empty spaces.
  • Pawns capture exactly 1 space horizontally or vertically.
  • Pawns never move diagonally and never capture diagonally.
  • Pawns may not capture a knight backward.

Knights

  • Knights jump straight forward only. They do not move diagonally.
  • A knight jumps over an adjacent pawn into the next seam line beyond it.
  • A knight may land on an empty point or capture an enemy pawn on the landing point.
  • Knights cannot land on any knight.
  • If another forward jump is available after landing, the knight continues chaining forward in the same move.

Scoring And Winning

  • Your home line is line 5 from your own side.
  • A knight counts as home if its jump path reaches line 5 or goes beyond it.
  • Getting 2 of your knights home wins immediately.
  • If both sides are down to 1 or fewer live knights, a side with 1 knight home beats a side with none.
  • If one side has the last remaining live knight, that side wins.
  • If a player has no legal moves, that player loses.
